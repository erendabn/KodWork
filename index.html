About therole / Segment

It's anexciting time here at Siemens Digital Industries. To support our growthIndustry 4.0 portfolio in both discrete and process industries, the PortfolioDevelopment department in Mexico is looking for a Portfolio Developer for theAerospace Industry.

Theobjective of the Portfolio Development function is to ensure that our salesorganization achieves assigned revenue targets for each solution in ourPortfolio. The Portfolio development role achieves the objective by creatingand maintaining plans for their assigned product or solution domain withintheir defined territory, aligning those plans with collaborators across sales,the segments and industry strategy. Further, they drive the execution of thoseplans through the appropriate sales functions.

Area ofresponsibility

  • Build and maintain a business plan for the specific portfolio elements that supports and aligns with the Country Plan, while ensuring these plans are aligned with marketing, sales, etc.
  • Identify and select the most appropriate global and local Campaigns for their chosen portfolio.
  • Use market analysis tools to identify suspect accounts (portfolio suspects).
  • Collaborate with a sales orchestrator or a tele-prospector for mature contacts for the suspects, and ultimately fill the pipeline with opportunities generated from the suspects.
  • Collaborate with our Partners for their enablement on the selected Campaigns and the development of suspect opportunities and, this way, boost their business on assigned portfolio.
  • Develop and engage in multiple opportunities, primarily in the identify and discover stage of the sales process.
  • Provide feedback and suggestions for improvements on specific local and global Campaigns as well as additional needs of their territory to the zone or global PfD.
  • Ensure adequate awareness and education for the entire chosen Portfolio within the country organization.
  • Working together to optimize production processes within the Digital Enterprise, focusing on improving efficiency, flexibility and time-to-market (more information: https://new.siemens.com/global/en/company/about/businesses/digital-industries.html)
